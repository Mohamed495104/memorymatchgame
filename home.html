Welcome to Memory Match!

Test your memory skills in this exciting card-matching game. Flip cards to reveal hidden images and find matching pairs. Complete the game to unlock beautiful images in the gallery and compete for the top spot on the leaderboard!

How to Play

  • Choose a difficulty level: Easy (4×3), Medium (4×4), or Hard (6×4).
  • Click on cards to reveal their images — you can flip two cards at a time.
  • If the images match, the cards stay flipped; if not, they flip back.
  • Match all pairs to win and earn points based on your moves and time.
  • Unlock gallery images and aim for a high score on the leaderboard!
